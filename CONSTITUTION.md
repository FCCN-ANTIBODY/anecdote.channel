## Mobile LLM

To allow for free text to be coerced easily for these rules, Anecdote delivers a heavily pruned mobile LLM for your browser, but you must run it with your device's power.

When operable at all, its included CONSTITUTION must intend to co-sign any submission where it becomes involved. The LLM will attest to not using an event loop for anything but User-confirmed actions, and confirmations must never be mandatory in the UX supporting its interactions with you.

If a fundamentally context-aware agent cannot rationalize Your Input, 1) it will not be collected and 2) you will be allowed to know it.

## Polls

Submissions to Anecdote must do all of the following to pass Moderation:

1. Be in the form of a question.
2. Prepare for a write-in response to be possible.
3. Can be posed and answered in translation.

## Responses

Replies to submissions must do all of the following to pass Moderation:

1. Not be interpreted purely as an interjection.
2. Use a fewest-verbs form to express its self-determined concept.
