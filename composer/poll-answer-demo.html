The poll-answer view — anecdote shaped by a Tell QR

The panel runs in a powerless data: chamber. The Elevated app reads the poll's QR (which was addressed to a Tell from the start) and hands the chamber the answer view over the probe line. The chamber shows the question and any suggested options — but the answer is always custom. Choosing or typing builds a pre-filled GitHub issue link; nothing phones home. All ops are Rung 0.
